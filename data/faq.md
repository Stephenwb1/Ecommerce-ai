# Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping

**How long does shipping take?**
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Expedited shipping (2–3 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. Overnight shipping is available for orders placed before 2pm EST.

**Do you ship internationally?**
We currently ship to the US and Canada only. International shipping is not available at this time.

**How much does shipping cost?**
Standard shipping is free on orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a flat $5.99 shipping fee. Expedited shipping is $12.99 regardless of order size.

**How do I track my order?**
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also look up your order status by providing your order ID to our support chatbot.

## Returns & Exchanges

**What is your return policy?**
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.

**How do I start a return?**
Contact us at support@fakestore.com with your order number and the items you'd like to return. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 1 business day.

**Do you offer exchanges?**
Yes. If you need a different size or color, mention it when you contact us and we'll process an exchange instead of a refund.

**When will I get my refund?**
Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of receiving the returned item. You'll get the refund to your original payment method.

## Orders

**Can I cancel or modify my order?**
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placing them. After that, the order has likely already been processed. Contact support@fakestore.com immediately if you need to make a change.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

## Contact

**How do I reach customer support?**
Email us at support@fakestore.com. We respond within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm EST).
