# Store Policies

## Warranty Policy

All clothing items are covered by a 90-day warranty against manufacturing defects (e.g., broken zippers, faulty stitching, defective hardware). This warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, damage from improper care, or accidental damage.

To make a warranty claim, email support@fakestore.com with your order number and a photo of the defect. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund at our discretion.

## Privacy Policy

We collect only the information needed to process your order: name, shipping address, email address, and payment information. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Payment information is processed securely through our payment provider and is never stored on our servers.

We may send you order confirmation and shipping notification emails. You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email we send.

## Terms of Service

By placing an order, you agree that:
- All sales are subject to product availability.
- Prices are subject to change without notice, but the price at time of order is locked in.
- We reserve the right to cancel any order due to pricing errors or suspected fraud.
- Disputes will be resolved under the laws of the State of Delaware.

## Size & Fit Policy

We provide size guides on each product page. If an item doesn't fit, you can return or exchange it within 30 days under our standard return policy — no questions asked.

## Sustainability

We are committed to reducing packaging waste. All orders ship in recycled or recyclable packaging. We do not use plastic poly mailers.
